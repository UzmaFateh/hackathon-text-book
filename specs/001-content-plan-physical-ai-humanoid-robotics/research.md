# Research Plan: Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics Content Outline

## Textbook Content Plan

### Chapter 1: Introduction to Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics
*   **1.1 What is Physical AI?**
    *   **AI Task**: Generate initial text defining physical AI and its distinction from virtual AI, in simple terms.
*   **1.2 What are Humanoid Robots?**
    *   **AI Task**: Generate initial text introducing humanoid robots, their general design, and primary purposes, avoiding technical jargon.
*   **1.3 Why Combine Them?**
    *   **AI Task**: Generate initial text explaining the basic benefits and challenges of merging physical AI with humanoid robotics, using straightforward language.
*   **1.4 A Brief History**
    *   **AI Task**: Generate initial text providing a concise overview of key historical moments in the development of AI and robotics.

    *   **AI Task**: Generate initial text detailing how robots use computer vision to "see" and interpret their surroundings.
